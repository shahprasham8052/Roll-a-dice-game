Welcome to the dice roll game

Instructions:-

  1. The game is played with a single six-sided die.
  2. Two players take turns in rolling the die.
  3. The player with the highest number on their roll wins the round.
  4. If there is a tie, roll again until one player has the higher number.
  5. The game continues until a predetermined number of rounds have been played or until one player reaches a predetermined number of points to win the game.
